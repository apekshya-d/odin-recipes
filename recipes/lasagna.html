Lasagna

Description

Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings such as meat, cheese, vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 3 each skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 (8 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 3 cloves garlic(chopped)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 25 ounce spaghetti sauce
  • 16 ounce ricotta cheese
  • Â½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil over medium-high heat and add chicken breasts. Let it shimmer for 10-15 minutes, drain water and shred the chicken with two forks.
  3. While chicken is boiling, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and lay noodles in a single, flat layer on a paper towel to dry.
  4. Heat oil in a small saute pan over medium-high heat. Saute onion and garlic just until onion is translucent, 5 to 7 minutes.
  5. Mix shredded chicken, onion-garlic mixture, 1 cup spaghetti sauce, ricotta cheese, 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning together in a large bowl.
  6. Mix remaining spaghetti sauce and remaining Parmesan cheese together in another bowl. Spread a thin layer on the bottom of a glass 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer 3 lasagna noodles, spread chicken mixture on top, then add another thin layer of the sauce mixture. Repeat, leaving a final layer of noodles on top. Top with remaining sauce. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheese bubbles, about 45 minutes.
